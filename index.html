My Favorite Anime

My personal list of top Anime, shows and movies.

  • Cowboy Bebop
  • Kotoro Lives Alone
  • Kaguya-Same: Love is War
  • Death Note
  • Mob Psycho 100

Cowboy Bebop

Why I like it: Cowboy Bebop was actually the first Anime I was exposed too. The dubbed version is good quality which makes a huge difference, and is great for people not used to reading subtitles. I quickly learned that good dubs are pretty rare. The story follows a group of bounty hunters in space, and has a very Old West feel. The storrytelling is excellent and I grew to really like each character.

Kotoro Lives Alone

Why I like it: The story doesn't make sense, it is basically a story about a 5 year old boy who live alone in an apartment. The neighbors help him out and gradually you learn about how the he came to live alone. Again, it doesn't make sense but despite that I appreciated all the lessons about doing the right thing and helping out your neighbor.

Kaguya-Same: Love is War

Why I like it: The basic premise is that the two main characters like eachother, are pretty sure the other likes them but due to their pride are trying to get the other to confess first which is to their detriment. The story is first very funny, but it is also sweet and charming. I was rooting for both of them and they each try to trick the other into confessing their feelings.

Death Note

Why I like it: This a thrilling story about a boy who gains a mysterious power and uses it to do "good", but the power corrupts him and he becomes the villain of the story. A game of cat and mouse ensues and each episode left me on the edge of my seat because I honestly don't know where the story would lead.

Mob Psycho 100

Why I like it: The main character, Mob, has what is likely the most powerful psychic/psionic abilities of all. However, Mob is endearing because although he could ace any test and be the best at any sport just by using his powers, he doesn't because he respects everyone else that has to work for all of their achievements and want to do the same. Through the story I found myself like and respecting Mob as he struggles to do the best he can without relying on his psychic abilities.
